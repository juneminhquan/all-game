A Special Dance in the Living Room

Lily grabbed her Dad's hand. "Let's dance!" she said with a big giggle. The music started, a happy song with a fun rhythm. Dad was her best dance partner. "I will lead, and you can follow," he said with a smile. They started with simple steps, a little shuffle to the left and a sway to the right. Lily loved to twirl, spinning around until she was dizzy.

The music's tempo got faster! They began to glide across the floor like skaters on ice. It took a lot of coordination to not bump into the sofa! Dad lifted her up high, and they spun together, laughing the whole time. It was the best dance ever.
